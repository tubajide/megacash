6 EASY WAYS TO OVERCOME YOUR WORRIES

Posted by scfundz 27th Apr 2020, 07:08am

Do they say don’t let the fear of losing be more than the excitement of winning then why worry?

Worry is actually the state or feeling of anxiety and unhappiness caused by the problems that you have or by thinking about unpleasant things that might happen.

Worry leads to overthinking which causes depression, Most people worry too much about different things like the present, the future, financial state and others.

Worry can’t be labelled as a good thing as there are ways to overcome it, Let’s take it to step by step:

1. Make up your mind to conquer

You can’t just let uncertain things bother you, buckle up to become a better person by not letting fear take over and consume you.

Face your fears so as to have a negative mindset to overcome any obstacle that comes to you.

2. Develop the right attitude of mind

A negative mind will never give you a positive life that’s one of the major reason many dreams have been destroyed due to doubt.

Bruce Lee once quoted “one of the best lessons you can learn in life is to master how to remain calm” and it has taught us never to think/do what drains us.

3. Do what you can and turn the rest to God

There is no substitute for hard work except hard work, Cutting corners and running from obstacles wouldn’t help so always try to put in your best effort, as a saying goes thus “heaven help those who help themselves”

4. Live one day at a time

Yesterday is buried and tomorrow is Unborn, A person can’t probably run faster than time.

Try to take everything one step at a time because nothing in life is to be rushed.

5. Engage in purposeful work

Don’t run around aimlessly, how do I engage in purposeful work? by grabbing every opportunity you see, do not slack around with every opportunity you see because opportunity only comes once in a while and make use of your time very well.

6. Re-focus your faith, restrict your thought and reset your priority

How do I re-focus my faith? by simply re-setting your faith to a higher level,.How do I restrict my thought?

By simply putting a barrier to every bad thought and by restricting every evil thought or decision and you must be determined to restrict your thought and must have faith and must believe in yourself.

And the last question is how do I reset my priority? you have to simply change your goal either long term or short term, you must have the mind and courage to chase the right goal\priority.
